BLAS Technical Workshop

May 20-22, 1996

Embassy Suites (Airport)

Atlanta, GA

We are organizing a workshop on May 20th and 22nd, 1996 to continue discussions on the BLAS. This will be a continuation to the discussions that have taken place on November 13th and 14th, 52 people attended a Workshop on Parallel BLAS and Related Interfaces at the University of Tennessee, the birds of a feather session that took place at the Supercomputer 95 meeting in San Diego in December, and the February 19-20, 1996 BLAST Forum Meeting in Nashville.
